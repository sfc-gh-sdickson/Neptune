1.  Which meter models have the highest anomaly rates, and what is the average age of those meters?
2.  What is the total water consumption for municipal utilities in California for the last quarter?
3.  Show me the top 5 utilities with the highest average customer satisfaction scores from completed work orders.
4.  Analyze the trend of total water consumption over the past 12 months. Is there a seasonal pattern?
5.  What is the average work order resolution time in hours for "Leak Investigation" tickets compared to "Meter Replacement" tickets?
6.  Which service technicians have completed the most high-priority work orders?
7.  Identify customers with a consistently high anomaly rate over the last 6 months who have not had a work order created.
8.  What is the distribution of our deployed meters by technology (AMI vs. AMR)?
